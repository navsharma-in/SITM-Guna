Admission Procedure Step By Step

Directorate of Technical Education, Madhya Pradesh, governs admission to all seats. Candidates seeking admissions in all branches of Engineering have to appear in the Online Off-Campus Counseling conducted by DTE, MP. The detailed process is available on website www:dtempcounselling.org and www.mponline.gov.in. Broadly the admission process involve the following steps:

  • Step 1: Document Verification
  • Step 2: Registration (For all candidates including unreserved)
  • Step 3: Choice Filling (For all candidates including unreserved)
  • Step 4: Allotment Letter (For all candidates including Unreserved)
  • Step 5: Reporting at Institutions (For all candidates including Unreserved)
    • Procedure
    B.E.Ist Year

    Admissions to the B.E Program would be made for the basis of Pre Engineering Entrance Test (PEET), conducted by Central Board of Secondary Education.

    General Seats

    The admission to 85% of the total seats (50% Uttarakhand and 35% All India) would be made through State level counselling conducted by Uttarakhand Technical University. The basis of this counselling is the rank obtained by the students in All India Engineering Entrance Examination (AIEEE) conducted by CBSE.
    For admission to the General category seats, Uttarakhand Technical University advertises the counselling schedule in the National dailies like Times of India, Amar Ujala and Dainik Jagran etc.

    Management Seats 15 % Management admissions are made directly by the Institute by conducting Institute level counselling based on AIEEE Rank. For Management category admissions, candidates need to register in the Institute. Registration forms can be obtained from our Institute, or could be downloaded from www.sitmguna.com.

    Candidate may get themselves registered by downloading the registration form . Along with the registration form complete in all respect, with the required documents (Mark sheets, etc) attach a DD of Rs. 500 in the name of "Quantum school of Technology" payable at Dehra Dun. The Documents and the DD can either be submitted to the institute by hand , or can be couriered to the following address:
    The Admissions Officer,
    SITM Campus,
    A.B.Road, Guna 400047.

    Admission for B.E IInd Year.

  • Lateral Entry

    • Seats Distributions : 10% of the total sanctioned intake would be reserved for the Diploma holders.

    Eligibility : Candidate should have passed the 3-4 years of Diploma after 10+ in any stream of Engineering and Technology with minimum of 60% aggregate or Graduate in B.Sc with 60% aggregate.

    Procedure for Lateral Entry : The admission to the B.Tech Second year through lateral entry would be made by the counselling organized by State Counselling Board, based on the rank obtained in the state entrance test UKSEE. And for lapsed seats, candidates need to register in the Institute. Registration forms can be obtained from the college.
    The registration fee is Rs 500 which is to be paid in the form of DD favouring "Quantum School of Technology" payable at Dehradun

    Fee Structure : The college charges fee which has been approved by the government of Uttarakhand. All fees have to be paid in advance during the time of admission, no adjustments, no instalments or refund is allowed. All fees have to be paid by demand draft in favour of "Quantum School of Technology" payable at DehraDun.
