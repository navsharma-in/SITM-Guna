Sakshi Institute of Technology & Management

By Raod

  • SITM, located on the outskirts of Guna on the main highway further towards Guna, is easily accessible by car through a network of national highways from Guna, Shivpuri, Ashoknagar, Biora,Etc..

    • By Air

  • Nearest airport to SITM is Guna's Guna airport which has regular flight services from Guna. The airport is under major expansions and shall very soon offer much diversified flight options.

    • By Train

  • Easiest way to get to SITM is by train. There are several trains serving Guna daily from various cities around.
