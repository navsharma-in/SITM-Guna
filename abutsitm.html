Sakshi Institute of Technology & Management

India keeps on nurturing a huge a pool of talents that believe in out-of-box thinking to bring revolution in the technology sector. The credit for producing such creative brains time and again must go to the top engineering institute in India. A few institutes in the country always strive to distinguish themselves from others with their ground-breaking teaching standards, courses and infrastructure.

The Prem Nagar Group started Sakshi Institute of Technology & Management with a vision to impart quality professional education of international standard through continuous up gradation of the curricula based on global industrial needs and to achieve professional excellence and human values through untiring collective efforts-thus transforming the budding professional into competent and skilled citizen to suit the Corporate World and achieve the mission- "Developed the Noble Cause entrusted to them as mentioned above"

There are both government and private engineering institutes at Bhopal as well as in the entire nation. All these Institute offer a wide range of full time undergraduate courses and programs with specialization in Computer Science, Information Technology, Mechanical, Electronics, Civil and Electrical Engineering. As the number of Institute and courses are sky rocketing, student find it hard to choose the right Institute and course purposefully suiting their career professionally. Before selecting any Institute the parents and the students must visit once the S.I.T.M. Guna to know about the infrastructure, the internal Facility, the quality policy and program, the best teaching faculties and fully residential girls and boys hostels which you will realize are the best amongst the various Engineer Institutes in M.P. and around.
