It is a matter of great pride and privilege for me and for those, who were incessantly associated with the institute, having a dream to impart the state of the art education to one and all.

I feel honoured that our efforts have paid through and today we have become, for which we have aspired, a institute in the name and style of Sakshi Institute of Technology. I am indeed very happy that we carry the name of PN group as our prefix for which I pledge that I along with my team will leave no stone unturned to make this name a globally known place for excellence in higher and technical education.

We have focused much of our past few years revising our strategic plans and on envisioning what an educational institution can become in the next five years. As with all planning exercises, we shall achieve some of our goals and re-evaluate others. And, given the dynamic nature of higher education, we will no doubt pursue some new innovations in those areas which are barely imaginative today.

The one constant will be our commitment to maintain SITM's role as an institution dedicated to diverse and ever expanding community that comprises students, scholars, academicians, corporate leaders and intellectuals of the city and region that we serve.

Our success will be measured by the number of students we educate and quality of education we provide. It will be reinforced by talented men and women who commit their professional lives to SITM as teachers, researchers and support staff who truly view college as a great place to work.

And last but not the least I thank all the students, their parents, the teachers, the staff and all those who were directly or indirectly associated with us and have made our current growth possible.

Mr. Premnarayan Rathore (Chairmen)

Education is a culture to nourish the knowledge by enlightening the spirit of dynamic and innovative professional skill. When education gets embedded with quality, the excellent human resource is generated for the man kind.

Sakshi Institute of Technology , Guna has been blessed with an aura of immense dedication and service to the education sector. It hereby promotes and preserves the pursuit of academic enrichment and inter-personal development. The environment that we cherish in SITM brings strength not only to teaching and research but also in other curriculum activities in order to transform the complete personality of teacher and taught.

We are proud of our efficient, professional and innovative workforce which is engaged in building the reputation of the Institutions in the group. The hillock campus with natural beauty, away from the crowed of the city, creates the learning environment.

Our noted achievements are the placements of our students in multinational companies, even during the recession, results and competitions won at national and international levels. These achievements have made the SITM a brand name in the area of technical and professional education.

Mr.Radhyeshyam Rathore (Director)
