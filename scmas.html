Message from Secretary

YOUR GATEWAY TO A BRIGHTER TOMORROW.

Cherish your visions and your dreams, as they are the children of your soul, the blueprints of your ultimate achievements".

Knowledge is acquired through education, information, intelligence and experience. It is available in academic institutions, with teachers, in libraries, in research papers, seminar proceedings and in workplaces. The Sakshi Institute of Technology and Management is a combination of all these. SITM brings new thoughts and new thoughts lead to innovative actions. You should ignite your dormant, inner energy by studying at SITM and let the University guide your lives. SITM is a premier, dynamic, vibrant professional and technical institution. The ambience of the college will be to your personal liking. The college challenges you to dream big, to aim for the stars and to put your heart into whatever you do.

SITM is lovable and livable. There is something about the SITM environment which many other institutions cannot match. There is no charm greater than studying at an institution with a pulsating heterogeneous crowd.

We have made a conscious effort to focus singularly on our areas of strength, which is quality affordable education for students of every stratum of society.

We welcome you to a centre of learning where you'll find the key to unlock the golden door of intellectual freedom and success........ This is where your story of glory begins, where your vision will clear and your actions will improve.......... We welcome you to Sakshi Institute of Technology and Management, Guna.

Er. Amit Rathore (secretary )
